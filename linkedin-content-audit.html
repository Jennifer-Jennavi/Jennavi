About This Tool

Understanding
Your Scores

How does the LinkedIn Content Audit Tool work?
Paste the text of your LinkedIn post and the tool automatically analyses it across four CRICKETS dimensions. Hook Quality checks whether your opening line creates recognition, discomfort, curiosity, or surprise. Pain Specificity checks whether you name a real specific problem your reader faces. Proof Presence checks whether you include numbers, results, or documented outcomes. Conversion Intent checks whether your post has a clear next step. Each dimension scores up to 25 points for a total of 100.
What is a good content score?
A score of 81 to 100 means your content is structured to convert. A score of 61 to 80 means good content with missing conversion elements. A score of 41 to 60 means average content that builds awareness but not revenue. A score of 21 to 40 means significant structural problems. A score of 0 to 20 means the content is unlikely to generate any client conversations regardless of how often you post it.
What is the CRICKETS framework?
CRICKETS is a 53-page LinkedIn positioning playbook created by Jennifer Omaliko of Jennavi. It documents the exact methodology she used to grow her own LinkedIn from 500 to 5,500 followers with 873% impression growth and zero paid ads. This content audit tool is built on the CRICKETS framework. Get CRICKETS for $29.90 at jennavi.co/book.html.
My score is low. What should I do?
A low content score usually points to one of three problems. Your hooks are informational rather than emotional. Your posts lack specific proof. Or your posts end without directing the reader to a next action. The good news is all three are fixable quickly.
