What is the Jennavi LinkedIn Health Check?
The Jennavi LinkedIn Health Check is a free 20-question quiz built on the 20-Point Brutal Profile Audit from the CRICKETS Manifesto by Jennifer Omaliko. It scores your LinkedIn across six categories: Banner, Headline, About Section, Featured Section, Content, and Positioning. You receive an instant personalised score and a specific Jennavi service recommendation.
How does the scoring work?
Each of the 20 questions is worth up to 5 points, giving a maximum of 100. Scores of 0 to 20 mean a complete rebuild is needed. Scores of 21 to 40 mean significant repositioning gaps. Scores of 41 to 60 mean posting but not converting. Scores of 61 to 80 mean visible but not converting at scale. Scores of 81 to 100 mean authority-level presence ready for VIP management.
