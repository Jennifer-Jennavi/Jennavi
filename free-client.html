Questions founders ask before applying

Is this really completely free with no strings attached?
Yes. No payment. No card details. No hidden fees. You receive the full Jennavi Starter tier service. In exchange Jennifer asks for documented results at the end of the month: numbers, screenshots, and a short testimonial. That is the only exchange. If you want to continue after the month it moves to a paid retainer. If you do not, there is no pressure.
What happens after the free month?
Nothing is automatic. If you want to continue, it moves to a paid retainer starting at $200 per month for the Starter tier. If you do not want to continue, the month stands on its own with no obligation. Jennifer will discuss options with you at the end of the performance review call.
How will the three founders be selected?
Jennifer reviews every application personally. The three founders selected will be those whose businesses, LinkedIn presence, and goals create the clearest opportunity for a visible and documented transformation. Submitting does not guarantee selection. Jennifer will contact the selected founders directly via email or WhatsApp.
How much time will this take from me?
Approximately 2 to 3 hours across the full month. One voice capture session at the start around 45 minutes. Content approvals within 24 hours, around 5 to 10 minutes per batch. A performance review call at the end around 30 minutes. Jennifer handles everything else.
What if I apply and am not selected?
Your application stays on file. You can also access the paid service at any time starting from a $100 one-time profile audit at jennavi.co/hire-linkedin-ghostwriter.html. Or get CRICKETS free at jennavi.co/free-crickets.html. Or use the four free LinkedIn tools at jennavi.co/linkedin-tools.html. There is no shortage of ways to start building right now.
What counts as a documented result?
Impression growth screenshots from LinkedIn analytics. Profile visit increases. Inbound DMs or inquiries received during the month. A short written testimonial about your experience. Jennifer will guide you on exactly what to capture throughout the month so nothing is missed.
