About This Tool

Understanding
Your Profile Score

How do I download my LinkedIn profile as a PDF?
Open LinkedIn on a desktop browser. Go to your profile page. Click the three dots next to the Add Section button. Click Save to PDF from the dropdown. LinkedIn generates and downloads the PDF instantly. This is only available on desktop. The mobile app does not have this feature. Upload the PDF to the Full Profile Audit at jennavi.co/linkedin-profile-audit.html for an instant score.
What does the Full Profile Audit score?
The Full Profile Audit scores four dimensions each worth 25 points. Headline: client naming, outcome clarity, and proof anchor. About Section: client clarity, outcome specificity, proof presence, and call to action. Experience: whether roles show specific numbers and outcome language versus task descriptions. Skills and Profile Signals: whether your top skills align with your positioning and whether completeness signals are present including a featured section, custom URL, and location.
What makes a LinkedIn headline effective for founders?
An effective headline scores on four dimensions: Client Specificity (naming who you serve precisely), Outcome Clarity (stating the specific result clients achieve), Clarity and Readability (understandable in under three seconds), and Scroll-Stop Power (memorable enough to pause the right person). The strongest headlines follow this structure: I help specific client achieve specific outcome through what you do. A job title headline like CEO at Company Name scores near zero on all four dimensions because it names nothing about the client, the result, or the transformation.
Why does my LinkedIn About section matter more than my posts?
Your About section is the only part of your LinkedIn profile a visitor reads with the intention of deciding whether to reach out. Posts are consumed and forgotten. Your About section is where the conversion decision is made. A weak About section means every impressive post you write sends people to a page that fails to close them. The About Section Audit scores four dimensions: Client Clarity, Outcome Specificity, Proof Presence, and Call to Action.
Is my LinkedIn PDF uploaded to a server?
No. The PDF parsing happens entirely in your browser using PDF.js. Your file is never sent to any server. The text is extracted locally, scored locally, and nothing leaves your device. This is a fully client-side tool.
